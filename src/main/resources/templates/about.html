The Problem We Solve

Every year, millions of professionals face a critical decision: Should I take this job offer in a new city?

The salary looks higher on paper. But after state taxes, housing costs, and local expenses—is it really a raise? Or are you just working harder to maintain the same lifestyle?

Most people guess. We calculate.

Our Approach

OfferVerdict uses a "Reverse-Disposable Income" methodology. Instead of just comparing nominal salaries, we:

  • Calculate actual tax burdens using 2025 IRS and state tax tables
  • Factor in real housing costs from Numbeo and local market data
  • Estimate living expenses based on city-specific cost indices
  • Show your true residual income—what you actually keep

Our Data Sources

We rely exclusively on authoritative, publicly available data:

  • IRS — Federal tax brackets and deductions (2025)
  • State Revenue Departments — State and local tax rates
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) — Wage data and occupational statistics
  • Numbeo — Cost of living indices and housing data
