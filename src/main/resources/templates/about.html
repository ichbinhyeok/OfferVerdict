About the pivot

OfferVerdict is a healthcare offer-risk tool.

It is a decision tool for healthcare workers who need to know whether a relocation offer is safe after taxes, rent, moving costs, shift assumptions, and bonus repayment clauses.

01

The old question was too broad

"Is this salary enough in this city?" sounds useful, but it is too broad. The harder decision is whether the written offer is safe once bonus repayment, relocation gaps, and shift-dependent upside are separated from base pay.

02

The new question has teeth

Healthcare relocation offers often look attractive because the bonus, stipend, overtime, and differentials are shown together. The risk is hidden in repayment language and non-guaranteed schedule assumptions. OfferVerdict pulls those apart.

What the tool is trying to do

The product gives a fast, practical read: acceptable, negotiate, or walk away. It is not a legal, tax, or financial advisor. It is a structured checklist and calculation layer before a worker signs a contract that may be expensive to leave.
