Why I Built This

A personal note from Gabi.

Hi, I'm Gabi.

A few years ago, I received what looked like a dream job offer. The salary was 30% higher than what I was making. I packed my bags, moved across the country, and expected my life to upgrade significantly.

I was wrong.

Between state taxes I hadn't calculated, a ridiculous increase in car insurance, and housing costs that were double what Zillow estimated, my "raise" effectively vanished. I was making more money on paper, but I felt poorer.

OfferVerdict is the Tool I Wish I Had

I built OfferVerdict to solve this specific pain point. Most "cost of living calculators" just give you a generic percentage match. They don't tell you about the local tax nuances, the specific housing market reality, or the wealth-building potential over 10 years.

My goal is simple: To help you make the best financial decision of your career, with eyes wide open.
