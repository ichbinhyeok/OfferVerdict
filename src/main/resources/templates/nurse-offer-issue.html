why it matters

A bonus can look generous while the contract puts exit risk on you.

The review should start from the actual written terms. A recruiter summary can point you in the right direction, but the decision changes only when the final offer confirms pay, unit, shift, float, cancellation, support, and repayment language.

Red flags to catch

  • The repayment clause is full instead of prorated.

Ask before you sign

  • Is repayment forgiven monthly?

What changes the decision

  • The written offer confirms the risk clearly.

What the tool needs

  • Offer letter or recruiter email

Walk-away line

If they will not write proration and termination protection into the final offer, treat the bonus as risk, not income.
